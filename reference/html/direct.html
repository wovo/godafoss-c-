direct

The direct<> decorator accepts an item and decorates it by inserting the appropriate refresh or flush before or after each read, write, or direction change operation, and replacing the refresh and flush operations by empty functions.

The effect is that such a decorated item can be used without refresh or flush calls. 

template< typename T >
concept can_direct = requires {
   item< T >;
};

template< typename T >
   requires can_direct< T >
struct direct< T > : ... ;
